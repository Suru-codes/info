Top 5 dog breeds !

-by Suraj Mokal

Whenever we talk about animals who are best companions to human beings, dogs are one of the first names which comes to our mind. They are very energetic, friendly and known for their loyal behaviour. Every dog is beautiful in its own away ❤️


So let's talk about few popular breeds of our four-pawed friends.


1. German Shepherd

This breed of dog is popular for its loyalty, courage and ability to learn new tasks.
German shepherds make one of the best family dogs and are also used as K-9 dogs in police force because of their intelligence and trainable nature.

2. Labrador Retriever

This breed of dog is famous for its all around friendliness nature with kids, family and other dogs.
Labrador's are active, intelligent and sensitive. What more to ask for a perfect family dog !

3. Siberian Husky

Siberian husky are unique because of their appealing look and different eye colors.
They tend to have a happy go lucky nature but sometimes behave stubborn.
Huskies are active, playful, friendly, affectionate but are not recommended as guard dogs.

4. Beagle

Beagle is a medium sized dog with long ears, this breed got fame after the movie My Dog Skip (2000).
They are very playful with kids, other dogs and are considered as one of the best family dogs !

5. Shih Tzu

The name Shih Tzu means little lion but there is nothing to be feared about this cute breed.
This breed is super active, playful, affectionate and if you need a partner who would follow you room to room then this dog is a Perfect choice.
